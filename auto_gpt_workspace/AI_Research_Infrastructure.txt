Key steps and facilities required for setting up an AI research company:
1. Encourage a collaborative approach between fundamental AI researchers and those applying AI solutions to real-world problems.
2. Provide a dynamic, comfortable, and unique workspace to attract talented AI researchers.
3. Offer compelling datasets and interesting problems for researchers to work on.
4. Ensure sufficient computational resources for the AI researchers.
5. Build a strong team for research mentorship and support.
6. Allow researchers to pursue curiosity-driven research and publish their results.
7. Promote open collaboration and transparency to advance the research community.
