Forbes Advisor provides a list of low-risk investments for conservative investors who want to protect their money from potential losses while still benefiting from modest growth. The article lists examples of such investments, which include U.S. Treasury securities, corporate bonds, money market mutual funds, fixed annuities, preferred stocks, common stocks that pay dividends, and index funds.
